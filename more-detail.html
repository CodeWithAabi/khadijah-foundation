About Our Madressa

About Our Foundation

Khadija Al-Kubra Welfare Foundation is a dedicated Islamic institution that provides education, housing, and care for orphan and poor girls. Our facilities include a masjid, hifz classes, academic education, hostel, and full sponsorship to build confident, knowledgeable individuals.

Khadijah-Tul-Kubra Girls School خدیجہ الکبریٰ گرلز اسکول

A full-time academic + Islamic school for orphan and poor girls, combining Deen and Dunya under one roof.

خدیجہ الکبریٰ اسکول ایک مکمل دینی و دنیاوی تعلیمی ادارہ ہے جو یتیم اور نادار بچیوں کے لیے قائم کیا گیا ہے۔

Spiritual Learning

Our students are immersed in an environment of Imaan, where Islamic teachings are nurtured alongside character-building.

Modern Facilities

Our modern classrooms and safe housing help girls grow in both academic and religious knowledge with confidence.

Supportive Education

Every orphan and poor girl receives full support, including meals, uniforms, books, and mentorship from caring teachers.

Jamiah Rasheediya (Girls - Hifz) جامعہ رشیدیہ (طالبات حفظ)

A sacred space where young girls memorize the entire Qur’an with Tajweed in a spiritually focused environment.

جامعہ رشیدیہ ایک دینی ادارہ ہے جہاں بچیاں روحانی ماحول میں قرآن پاک مکمل حفظ کرتی ہیں۔

Qur’an Memorization

Young girls are trained to memorize the entire Qur’an with proper Tajweed under the guidance of expert female teachers.

Peaceful Learning Environment

Our dedicated halls create a spiritually focused atmosphere where students connect deeply with the Qur’anic journey.

Character & Discipline

Alongside memorization, students are taught Islamic manners, discipline, and a love for the Deen from a young age.

Ahsan-ul-Madaris (Boys - Hifz) احسن المدارس (حفظ لڑکے)

A growing school dedicated to boys' Qur’an memorization, focusing on discipline, character, and Tahfeez.

احسن المدارس ایک ترقی پذیر ادارہ ہے جہاں لڑکوں کو نظم و ضبط اور حفظِ قرآن کی تعلیم دی جاتی ہے۔

Qur’an Memorization

Our boys memorize the Holy Qur’an with Tajweed in a nurturing and disciplined Islamic environment guided by experienced teachers.

Islamic Character Building

We shape the future Ummah through strong Islamic manners, discipline, and regular supervision from scholars.

Brotherhood & Support

The madressa fosters brotherhood, mutual respect, and teamwork among students to help them grow together in Deen and discipline.

Masjid Ali-Ul-Murtaza مسجد علی المرتضیٰ

The central masjid of our foundation, serving as a hub for community prayers, sermons, and gatherings.

مسجد علی المرتضیٰ ہماری فاؤنڈیشن کا مرکز ہے جہاں نماز، بیانات اور دینی پروگرامز کا انعقاد ہوتا ہے۔

Central Prayer Hall

The masjid hosts all five daily prayers and the Jumu'ah congregation, uniting the community in worship and reflection.

Islamic Gatherings & Sermons

Weekly bayans and Islamic programs are held here to strengthen the spiritual roots of our community and youth.

Community Center

Beyond salah, the masjid serves as a community hub for learning, unity, and collective care through events and outreach.
